Welcome to the ordering page for Honeybee Bakery

All orders are for the next business day and are to be paid on arrival at our store.

Orders must be scheduled at least 48 hours in advance, and scheduled for 6:00am-6:00pm Monday-Friday.

We cannot currently take weekend orders due to high demand on weekends.

Available to order ahead of time:

    Full Cakes - $50

    Bread Loaves - $20

    Muffin Platters (12 muffins) - $50

    Catering-sized cofee and pastry orders (Orders of 6 or more coffees should be ordered ahead of time) - $15/person

Please order online or come in to our store to order in person.
